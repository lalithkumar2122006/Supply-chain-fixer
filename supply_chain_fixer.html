About this simulation — agent roles & fixer logic
Supplier produces raw material each day at a fixed capacity, and can suffer a delay disruption that cuts its output.
Manufacturer converts raw material into finished goods and can suffer a quality defect that wastes part of a day's production.
Distributor moves finished goods toward the retailer and can suffer a breakdown that cuts its shipping capacity.
Retailer sells to customers against daily demand, which can suddenly spike and outstrip inventory.

The Fixer agent runs after every other agent each day. It inspects each node's state and, if it finds a problem, applies one of four fixed responses: switch to a backup supplier, expedite a raw-material shipment, reroute a shipment through an alternate carrier, or emergency-restock the retailer from distributor buffer stock. Every fix is logged with the day it happened and the reasoning behind it, so the log doubles as an audit trail for the presentation.
